Triple Diamond Glass West Coast ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website https://www.triplediamondglassproducts.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, email address, service type, and project details when you submit a contact form or request a quote on our website.

We use your information to respond to service inquiries, provide estimates, schedule appointments, send job completion notifications, request Google reviews after service is completed, and send SMS messages with your explicit consent.

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent checkbox on our website form, you expressly consent to receive text messages from Triple Diamond Glass West Coast at the phone number provided.

  • Message & Data Rates: Msg & data rates may apply depending on your carrier and plan.

SMS consent is collected through the website form at https://www.triplediamondglassproducts.com. The consent checkbox is optional and not pre-checked. Opting in to SMS is not a condition of receiving our services. Consent applies only to communications from Triple Diamond Glass West Coast and is not transferable.
